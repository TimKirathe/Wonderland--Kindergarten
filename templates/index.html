Why Choose Us?

Experience

Now in our fifth decade of service, we have established a reputation for excellence, in providing superior education for young children. We do this in an atmosphere of Christian love and culture, based on Biblical values.

Academic Excellence

Today, our teachers are trained in the new curriculum and continue to eagerly prepare our young learners to be effective and productive members of our society through competency based learning.

Co-Curriculum

At Wonderland your child will never lack something to do. We offer a wide range of extra-curricular activities like abacus, swimming, football, skating, music, ballet and more!
